Common Cyber Threats

Major Attack Categories

Understanding common cyber attack methods helps improve digital awareness and security readiness.

🛡️

Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information using fake emails, websites, or messages.

💻

Malware

Malicious software designed to damage systems, steal files, or disrupt operations.

🔐

Ransomware

Attackers encrypt important data and demand payment to restore access.

🌐

DDoS Attacks

Overwhelming servers or networks with traffic to disrupt services and accessibility.

About Cyber Security

Cyber security focuses on protecting systems, networks, applications, and data from unauthorized access and digital attacks.

Modern organizations rely heavily on cyber defense strategies including encryption, threat monitoring, multi-factor authentication, and secure infrastructure management.

As technology continues to evolve, cyber awareness becomes increasingly important for maintaining privacy, operational continuity, and digital trust.

Security Practices

Protection Strategies

Applying strong cyber security practices significantly reduces digital risks.

🔑

Strong Passwords

Use complex passwords with a combination of numbers, symbols, and uppercase letters.

📲

Two-Factor Authentication

Enable additional authentication layers to secure accounts and sensitive systems.

☁️

Data Backup

Maintain regular backups to reduce data loss during cyber incidents.

⚡

Software Updates

Keep systems and applications updated to patch security vulnerabilities.
